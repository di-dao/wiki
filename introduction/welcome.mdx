With Sonr, developers can easily integrate <Tooltip tip="DID documents are a decentralized, self-sovereign method of storing and sharing identity information.">DID Documents</Tooltip>, <Tooltip tip="DID documents are a decentralized, self-sovereign method of storing and sharing identity information.">WebAuthn</Tooltip>, and <Tooltip tip="DID documents are a decentralized, self-sovereign method of storing and sharing identity information.">IPFS</Tooltip> to provide users with a secure, user-friendly way to manage their digital
identity and assets. Whether you're building a decentralized application, a social network, or a financial platform, Sonr has the tools you need to focus on shipping an engaging user experience.
