title: "ADR-007: Universal Communication Mesh"
description: This document presents a proposal for the Universal Communication Mesh, which aims to provide an open transport agnostic communication mechanism. The objective is to enable accessibility for users to listen to channels based on applications and allow developers to create channels for their individual development needs. The proposal includes details on addressing and identifying topics, client-side interaction, and the relationship between channel instances and topics. It also mentions the economic impact, including network fees and staking incentives. The implementation of this proposal is currently under development by the core Sonr Team.
